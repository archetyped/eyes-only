Eyes Only is the ultimate tool for seamless site privacy

Automatic Redactions let you hide your private thoughts in plain sight!

Whenever a visitors views a post, Eyes Only will find the words and phrases that you want to keep private and replace them with anything you want (e.g. replace someone's real name with a psuedonym, etc.). You or any authorized (logged-in) user will magically see the post in its full unmodified glory!

Adding an Automatic Redaction

  1. Click the "Add new" button
  2. Enter the text you would like to redact (hide) in your site's content in the "Text" field
  3. Enter the replacement text to use in place of the original text in the "Replacement" field
  4. Click the "Add" button

Editing an Automatic Redaction

  1. Move your mouse to the row you would like to edit and click the "Edit" link
  2. Enter the text you would like to redact (hide) in your site's content in the "Text" field
  3. Enter the replacement text to use in place of the original text in the "Replacement" field
  4. Click the "Save" button

Deleting an Automatic Redaction

  1. Move your mouse to the row you would like to edit and click the "Delete" link
  2. Click the "Yes" linked when asked to confirm deletion of the item
